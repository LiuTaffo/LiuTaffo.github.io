My Services

Visual Design

My main area of expertise is the user interface for iOS devices like iPhone and iPad. I really love the Apple Method, keep it simple and the easy way to the user. Every little piece count. Inclusive the icons, so tiny but soooo important. Icons must be ease to identification for its purpose and have to fit in pixel perfect.

UX

User Experience is the key for all project thrive and evolve. Begins with UX research to understand the user's needs, improve experiences, based on both quantitative, qualitative and competitors research. It is really important a keen eye in drawing the flow and wireframe always keeping in mind that the user must find the way to its aims without any effort. In a simple and quick manner to reach the goal.

Prototype

If you have the most amazing design, stunning colours and clarify icons and do not have a good prototype to run through it for your client, it will all go downhill. Us as a designer need to prototype like it's real. Sometimes is hard for the client visualize how it will going to be.

Wireframe

Even for us designers is hard to visualize the final project without doing it in a paper with a pencil and eraser. Yes, you read pencil and eraser lol... Our first sketches are drawing in this way to visualize everything and to not miss anything, at this phase, at least for me, I have lots of insights to move on to the next stages. After the paper, we do it on a computer :D

Colours

Colours are one of the main key. Using effective colours in your design can which evoke lots of reactions. Something as simple as changing the exact hue or saturation of a colour can evoke a completely different feeling. Cultural differences can compound those effects, with a hue that’s happy and uplifting in one country becoming depressing in another.

User Flow

The basis to understand the users needs. Is one of the first puzzle pieces of an history. For instance, I would like you to read all the services that I can provide to you. This way, you will understand what does the flow mean or even better, how my work flow works. If you do that, surely you will know about what a user flow is.
